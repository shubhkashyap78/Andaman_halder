06:00 AM · Departure
Phoenix Bay Jetty
Port Blair

Board MV Nautika at Phoenix Bay International Jetty — Andaman's main ferry terminal. Check in 30 minutes before departure. The ferry sets sail into the Bay of Bengal just as the sun rises over the harbour. Watch the Port Blair skyline fade into the ocean mist as you begin your island journey.

📍 Phoenix Bay Jetty
🕕 Boarding at 5:30 AM
🧳 Luggage Drop
☕ Sea Café Opens
~1 Hour · At Sea
Open Ocean
Bay of Bengal

Cruise through the open Bay of Bengal — this is the heart of the crossing. From the upper observation deck, watch flying fish leap alongside the hull, spot distant islands on the horizon, and feel the warm salty wind. The sea is calm for most of the year; the monsoon season (June–September) can bring gentle swells that some passengers find exciting.

🐟 Flying Fish Spotting
🌅 Open Deck Views
🍱 Snack Service
📸 Best Photo Opportunity
~08:00 AM · 1st Stop
Havelock Island
Swaraj Dweep

First landfall — Havelock Island, home to Asia's finest beach. Passengers for Havelock disembark at Havelock Jetty (Jetty No. 1). From here, resorts, Radhanagar Beach, and Elephant Beach are all within 10–30 minutes. Passengers continuing to Neil Island remain on board for the next leg of the journey.

🏖️ Radhanagar Beach
🤿 Elephant Beach
⏱️ ~2 hrs from Port Blair
🏨 Resort Transfers
~09:00 AM · Final Stop
Neil Island
Shaheed Dweep

Final destination — Neil Island, the most serene and unhurried island in the Andamans. Arrive at Neil Jetty and step into the slow pace of island life. Bharatpur Beach with its natural coral bridge is a 10-minute walk. Laxmanpur Beach for the famous bioluminescent plankton experience is a short auto ride away. Return ferry departs at 2:00 PM daily.

🪸 Coral Bridge
✨ Bioluminescent Beach
⏱️ ~3 hrs from Port Blair
🔄 Return at 2:00 PM

Everything You Need
at Sea
☕
Sea Café

Hot chai, coffee, sandwiches, samosas, and fresh fruit available throughout the journey. Pay-per-item for Economy; complimentary for Business & Premium.

🌬️
Full AC Interior

All lower and middle deck interiors are fully air-conditioned. Stay cool even as the Andaman sun climbs overhead on the open sea.

🔭
Observation Deck

The open upper deck offers 360° views of the Andaman Sea. Watch dolphins, flying fish, and distant island silhouettes as you sail.

🛡️
Safety Certified

MV Nautika is certified by the Indian Coast Guard. Life jackets, safety drills, and trained crew make every crossing safe and secure.

📶
Wi-Fi On Board

Stay connected with on-board Wi-Fi — available on Business and Premium class. Perfect for sharing those golden sunrise moments live.

🚻
Clean Restrooms

Hygienic, well-maintained restrooms on all decks. Business and Premium passengers have access to dedicated, private restroom facilities.

🛄
Luggage Storage

Secure luggage storage bay available on the lower deck. Travel light on deck and stow your bags safely for the duration of the crossing.

🌅
Sunrise Crossing

The 6:00 AM departure guarantees a spectacular open-sea sunrise. The Andaman horizon at dawn is one of the most beautiful sights in all of India.
