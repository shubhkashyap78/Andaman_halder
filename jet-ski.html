Why Choose Jet Skiing?

Jet skiing is the ultimate adrenaline rush for thrill-seekers! Commanding a personal watercraft across Andaman's open waters offers unmatched speed and freedom. Whether you're an experienced rider or trying for the first time, our professional instructors ensure safety while maximizing excitement. Jet skis give you complete control to explore pristine coves, hidden beaches, and open waters at your own pace. The combination of speed, maneuverability, and the natural beauty of Andaman's coastline creates an unforgettable experience. It's the perfect activity for those seeking action-packed adventures and independence on the water.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need experience?+
No prior experience needed! Our instructors provide comprehensive training before you start. We offer a safety orientation, equipment familiarization, and guided practice in shallow waters. You'll learn throttle control, turning, and emergency procedures in a safe environment.
Is jet skiing safe?+
Yes! All jet skis are modern, well-maintained, and equipped with: life jackets, helmets, kill switches, GPS, and safety radios. Our guides monitor all riders and maintain designated boundaries. You get comprehensive training and are never far from support.
What's the weight/age limit?+
Minimum age is 12-16 years (depending on jet ski model). Riders must weigh 40-140 kg. Teens 12-18 require adult supervision. Seniors (70+) should consult about fitness. Pregnant women, people with heart conditions, or severe back issues should avoid jet skiing.
What should I wear?+
Wear swimwear or water-resistant clothes. We provide: life jackets, helmets, and wetsuit options. Apply waterproof sunscreen liberally. Avoid jewelry and valuables. Water shoes are optional. Bring extra clothes for after the ride as you'll get wet.
How fast do the skis go?+
Modern jet skis can reach speeds of 40-60 km/h. However, we regulate speeds based on skill level: beginners stay 20-30 km/h, intermediate 30-45 km/h, experienced 45-60+ km/h. You control your own speed and adventure intensity!
Can I ride alone?+
All riders start with guided tours where an instructor on another jet ski accompanies you. After training and assessment, you may ride independently within designated boundaries. We always maintain visual contact for safety.
