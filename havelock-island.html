Havelock Island

White sand beaches, coral reefs, and crystal-clear lagoons make Havelock the most loved island in Andaman.

Overview

Havelock Island is perfect for beach lovers and water sports enthusiasts. It is famous for Radhanagar Beach, scuba diving, and serene sunsets.

Quick Facts

  • Best Time: October to May
  • Top Spots: Radhanagar Beach, Elephant Beach
  • Activities: Scuba, snorkeling, kayaking
  • Ideal Stay: 2 to 3 nights
BeachesSunset views and calm waters for a relaxed day.
