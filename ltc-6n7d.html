Day 01 · Arrival
Port Blair — Welcome to the Islands

Land at Veer Savarkar International Airport and meet your Andaman Tours representative. Check into your hotel and freshen up. Head to the historic Cellular Jail — a UNESCO tentative heritage site — and spend the evening at the emotionally stirring Light & Sound Show that narrates the stories of India's bravest freedom fighters. End with a peaceful stroll along Aberdeen Bazaar.

✈️ Airport Pickup
🏛️ Cellular Jail
🎭 Light & Sound Show
🚶 Aberdeen Bazaar Walk
Day 02 · Deep Jungle
Baratang Island — Nature's Hidden Wonders

Depart at 4:30 AM through the protected Jarawa Tribal Reserve — a convoy drive through primeval rainforest unlike anything in India. At Baratang, board a motorboat and glide through dense mangrove creeks to the spectacular Limestone Caves, naturally sculpted over millennia. Trek to the bubbling Mud Volcano — one of India's rarest geological phenomena. Return to Port Blair by evening, tired and thrilled.

🌿 Jarawa Reserve Convoy
🚤 Mangrove Creek Boat
🪨 Limestone Caves
🌋 Mud Volcano Trek
Day 03 · History & Reef
Ross Island & North Bay — Ruins, Reefs & Colours

Post breakfast, take a boat to Ross Island — the crumbling British administrative capital now peacefully reclaimed by deer, peacocks, and strangler figs. Wander its eerie ruins and visit the small museum. Sail on to North Bay Island for world-class snorkeling over brilliant live coral. Take the glass-bottom boat for a dry window into the underwater world. Afternoon free at the Port Blair seafront — try the local fish curry.

🏚️ Ross Island Ruins
🦚 Wildlife Spotting
🤿 North Bay Snorkeling
🐠 Glass-Bottom Boat
Day 04 · Havelock
Havelock Island — Asia's Best Beach & First Dive

Morning speed boat to Havelock Island (Swaraj Dweep). Check into your beachside resort. Head to the world-famous Radhanagar Beach — white sand, impossibly blue water, and swaying palms. After lunch, head to Elephant Beach for an exhilarating introductory scuba diving session — get face-to-face with turtles, parrotfish, and coral gardens at 5–8 metres depth. Watch the Havelock sunset from your beach resort. Overnight stay.

🚤 Speed Boat
🏖️ Radhanagar Beach
🤿 Elephant Beach
🐟 Intro Scuba Diving
Day 05 · Neil Island
Neil Island — Coral Bridges, Silence & Glowing Seas

Morning boat to Neil Island (Shaheed Dweep) — the quietest, most untouched of the Andaman islands. Walk the iconic natural coral bridge at Bharatpur Beach. Cycle or auto-rickshaw to Sitapur Beach for a dramatic sunrise viewpoint. In the evening, visit Laxmanpur Beach for its legendary sunset, then stay after dark for the rare bioluminescent plankton — the sea literally glows blue when touched. Overnight on Neil Island.

🪸 Natural Coral Bridge
🌅 Sitapur Sunrise Point
🌊 Laxmanpur Sunset
✨ Bioluminescent Beach
Day 06 · Diglipur & Departure
Diglipur — Sea Turtles, Saddle Peak & Farewell

Early morning flight or ferry to Diglipur — the northernmost and most wild part of Andaman. Visit Ross & Smith Island (connected by a natural sand bar) and the nesting beach at Kalipur where Leatherback and Green Sea Turtles come ashore. Hike a portion of Saddle Peak — the highest point in Andaman at 732 metres — for panoramic rainforest views. Return to Port Blair by afternoon and transfer to the airport. Depart with memories that will last a lifetime.

🏝️ Ross & Smith Island
🐢 Sea Turtle Beach
⛰️ Saddle Peak Hike
✈️ Departure
Day 07 · Leisure
Port Blair — Relaxation & Local Culture

Enjoy a relaxed morning with breakfast at your hotel. Explore local markets, historic sites like the Anthropological Museum, and optional activities such as a sunset cruise or spa session. Prepare for the final departure day with a romantic dinner by the sea.

What Makes 6N7D
The Ultimate Choice
🐟
01
Introductory Scuba Diving

Exclusive to 6N7D — take your first breath underwater at Elephant Beach. PADI-certified instructors guide you to vivid coral gardens at 5–8m depth. No experience needed.

✨
02
Bioluminescent Beach at Night

Neil Island's Laxmanpur Beach glows electric blue after dark — a natural light show caused by bioluminescent plankton. One of nature's most magical spectacles, anywhere on Earth.

🐢
03
Sea Turtle Nesting Beach

Diglipur's Kalipur Beach is one of India's last great turtle nesting sites. Watch Leatherback and Green Sea Turtles in their natural habitat — a privilege few travelers experience.

⛰️
04
Saddle Peak — Andaman's Roof

Trek through dense rainforest to the highest point in Andaman & Nicobar — 732 metres above sea level — for sweeping views of islands, sea, and untouched forest stretching to the horizon.

🏝️
05
Ross & Smith Twin Islands

Two islands connected by a pristine natural sandbar that appears and disappears with the tide — walk across it during low tide for one of Andaman's most dramatic and photogenic experiences.

🌿
06
Jarawa Tribal Reserve Drive

A convoy drive through the protected Jarawa forest at dawn — ancient rainforest, jungle sounds, and the rare chance to see one of the world's last isolated indigenous communities from a respectful distance.
