Day 01 · Arrival
Port Blair — Into the Island World

Arrive at Veer Savarkar International Airport, Port Blair. Our representative will receive you with a warm welcome and transfer you to the hotel. After check-in and rest, visit the legendary Cellular Jail — a sobering and deeply moving reminder of India's freedom struggle. End the evening with the powerful Light & Sound Show inside the jail premises.

✈️ Airport Pickup
🏛️ Cellular Jail
🎭 Light & Sound Show
🏨 Hotel Check-in
Day 02 · Adventure
Baratang Island — Caves, Creeks & Volcanoes

Early morning drive through the lush Jarawa Tribal Reserve forests to reach Baratang. Board a motorboat through dense mangrove creeks to reach the spectacular Limestone Caves — a geological wonder formed over thousands of years. Then trek to the rare natural Mud Volcano, one of only a handful in India. Return to Port Blair by late afternoon, carrying nature's surprises with you.

🌿 Jarawa Reserve Drive
🚤 Mangrove Creek Boat
🪨 Limestone Caves
🌋 Mud Volcano Trek
Day 03 · History & Sea
Ross Island & North Bay — Heritage Meets Ocean

After breakfast, board a boat to Ross Island — once the grand British administrative capital, now gloriously reclaimed by deer, peacocks, and jungle vines. Explore its eerie ruins and colonial architecture. Continue to North Bay Island for world-class snorkeling in vibrant coral gardens. Enjoy the glass-bottom boat ride for an underwater window to the reef. Return to Port Blair for a relaxed evening by the harbour.

🏚️ Ross Island Ruins
🦚 Wildlife Spotting
🤿 North Bay Snorkeling
🐠 Glass-Bottom Boat
Day 04 · Havelock
Havelock Island — Asia's Most Beautiful Beach

Morning speed boat to Havelock Island (Swaraj Dweep). Drop bags at your beachside resort and head straight to Radhanagar Beach — awarded Asia's Best Beach by Time Magazine, and you'll understand why the moment you set foot on it. Later, visit Elephant Beach for thrilling snorkeling, sea-walking, and water sports. Watch the sunset over the Bay of Bengal. Overnight stay in Havelock.

🚤 Speed Boat
🏖️ Radhanagar Beach
🤿 Elephant Beach
🛶 Sea Walk & Water Sports
Day 05 · Neil Island & Farewell
Neil Island — Quiet Coves & Coral Bridges

Morning boat to Neil Island (Shaheed Dweep) — calmer, greener, and even more unhurried than Havelock. Walk across the famous natural coral bridge at Bharatpur Beach and soak in the silence at Laxmanpur Beach, known for jaw-dropping sunsets. Savour fresh coconut water and local seafood at the market. Continue to Port Blair by afternoon and transfer to the airport for your onward flight — hearts full, cameras full.

🌴 Neil Island
🪸 Natural Coral Bridge
🌅 Laxmanpur Sunset
