07:00 AM · Departure
Haddo Jetty
Port Blair

Green Ocean departs from Haddo Jetty — Port Blair's secondary terminal, typically less crowded than Phoenix Bay. Board 30 minutes early, collect your eco-wristband, and take your seat as the ferry glides out of the harbour into open Andaman waters. The crew briefs passengers on the vessel's green practices — no plastic, no dumping, silent engine zones near coral reefs.

~45 min · At Sea
Coral Passage
Andaman Sea

Green Ocean slows through the protected coral corridor — a unique practice that sets it apart from conventional ferries. Engines are throttled down to reduce turbulence above the reef systems below. This is the best time to head to the bow deck: spot sea turtles surfacing, watch white terns dive, and photograph the emerald water turning deep sapphire blue as the islands fall behind.

🐢 Turtle Spotting
🪸 Reef Safe Zone
🌊 Open Deck Views

~09:00 AM · 1st Stop
Havelock Island
Swaraj Dweep

Green Ocean docks at Havelock Jetty — Asia's best beach destination. Passengers disembark here for Radhanagar Beach, Elephant Beach snorkeling, and resort stays. The vessel pauses for 20 minutes for resupply — all goods loaded follow the ferry's zero-waste sourcing policy. Passengers continuing to Neil Island enjoy a final stretch of open water with stunning views of Havelock's forested coastline.

🏖️ Radhanagar Beach
🤿 Elephant Beach
⏱️ ~2 hrs from Port Blair
🌴 Eco Resort Transfers
~10:00 AM · Final Stop
Neil Island
Shaheed Dweep

Final landfall at Neil Island — the quietest and most ecologically pristine island in the Andaman chain. Green Ocean's arrival is greeted by the island's famous natural coral bridge visible from the jetty. Explore Bharatpur, Laxmanpur, and Sitapur beaches at your own pace. Return ferry departs at 1:30 PM daily — arrive 15 minutes early at Neil Jetty.

🪸 Coral Bridge
✨ Bioluminescent Beach
⏱️ ~3 hrs from Port Blair

On-Board Experience
Everything Aboard
Green Ocean
🌿
Eco Certification

India's first Andaman ferry with an active eco-certification — low-emission engines, coral-safe fuel, and zero single-use plastic on board.

🥤
Organic Snack Bar

Fresh coconut water, local fruit, herbal teas, and island-sourced snacks. No packaged junk. Every item served in compostable packaging.

🌅
Bow Deck Sunrise

The open bow deck at 7 AM offers one of the most stunning sunrises on the Andaman Sea — warm gold over turquoise water, no crowds.

🐬
Dolphin Watch Zone

Green Ocean's designated bow observation zone lets passengers spot spinner dolphins and flying fish that regularly accompany the vessel.

🌬️
Full AC Interior

All enclosed decks are fully air-conditioned with energy-efficient systems. Cool, clean, and quiet — perfect for the 2-hour crossing.

🛡️
Coast Guard Certified

Certified by the Indian Coast Guard. All life-saving equipment is regularly inspected, and trained crew conduct a safety briefing before every departure.

♿
Accessibility Friendly

Green Ocean has dedicated accessible boarding, ramp access, and priority seating for elderly passengers, differently-abled travellers, and families with infants.

📵
Silent Reef Zones

Engine noise is minimised as the vessel crosses known coral reef zones — reducing underwater sound pollution. A quiet, conscious crossing through living ecosystems.
