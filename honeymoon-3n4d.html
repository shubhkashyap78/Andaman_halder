Day 01 · Arrival
Port Blair — Welcome to Paradise

Arrive at Veer Savarkar International Airport, Port Blair. Our representative will greet you and transfer you to your hotel. Post check-in and some rest, head out to the iconic Cellular Jail for a powerful Light & Sound show in the evening — a tribute to India's freedom fighters.

✈️ Airport Pickup
🏛️ Cellular Jail
🎭 Light & Sound Show
🏨 Hotel Check-in
Day 02 · Islands
Ross Island & North Bay — Sea, History & Coral

After breakfast, board a boat to Ross Island — once the administrative capital of British India, now a hauntingly beautiful ruin reclaimed by nature. Continue to North Bay Island for snorkeling and a thrilling glass-bottom boat ride over living coral reefs. Return to Port Blair by evening and enjoy a leisurely seafront dinner.

🏝️ Ross Island
🤿 Snorkeling
🐠 Glass-Bottom Boat
🌊 North Bay
Day 03 · Havelock
Havelock Island — Asia's Best Beach

Early morning speed boat to Havelock Island (Swaraj Dweep). Check in to your beach resort and head straight to Radhanagar Beach (Beach No. 7) — voted Asia's best beach by Time Magazine. Later, explore Elephant Beach for more snorkeling and water sports. Spend the night in Havelock amidst the sound of waves.

🚤 Speed Boat
🏖️ Radhanagar Beach
🤿 Elephant Beach
🛶 Water Sports
Day 04 · Departure
Neil Island Stop & Fond Farewell

Return to Port Blair via Neil Island (Shaheed Dweep) for a short stopover. Walk along the natural coral bridge at Bharatpur Beach and enjoy fresh coconut water. Continue to Port Blair by afternoon. Post lunch, transfer to the airport for your onward journey — carrying memories of turquoise waters and salty breezes forever.

🌴 Neil Island
🪸 Coral Bridge
🥥 Bharatpur Beach

Zip between Ross, Havelock, and Neil islands on fast speed boats — each island uniquely beautiful and culturally distinct from the last.
